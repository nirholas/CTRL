To use CTRL in a mobile or a vertically screened device with a dynamic viewport, consider installing the OS as an app.

## Installing CTRL as an App in Chrome

Chrome, or its alternatives like Edge, Opera, have mostly the same methods.

### Open Chrome

From your default OS.

### Go to [ctrl.best](https://ctrl.best)

and wait until CTRL installs.

Once it's done, and you see a dock,

### Tap the menu button

on Chrome; you can find it in the top right corner outside CTRL.

### Tap "Install CTRL" or "Add to home screen"

Now wait for your OS to make a dialog for its installation, install CTRL, and you are done.

### From the app menu on your device, open CTRL.

You now have your personal Web System ready to be used.
